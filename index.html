Why this structure works

We follow a layered architecture: global common assets, product-level common assets, and page-specific extensions.

Global common

Tokens, utilities, header/footer injection, accessibility and SEO helpers are centralized.

Product common

Each product gets its own header/footer and style behavior without duplicating global code.

Page node

Page-specific scripts/styles stay isolated, so maintenance and rollout remain predictable.
